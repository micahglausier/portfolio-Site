My Story

Micah Glausier - Full Stack Web Developer

I am deeply passionate about problem-solving and utilizing my unique skill set to assist others. My affinity for collaborative teamwork, where I can continuously learn and grow, has drawn me towards the field of Web Development. I find immense satisfaction in the process of identifying and resolving issues within projects, whether they are in their infancy, require maintenance, or are in a state of disrepair. This drive to excel extends to all facets of my life, as I strive to serve as a powerful source of inspiration for my children and those in my circle. My career journey initially led me into the realm of 501c3 organizations, where I spent a fulfilling 12 years. During this time, I delved into coding as a personal pursuit and quickly developed a profound affection for it. Currently, I am immersed in the Full Stack Program at CareerFoundry, and I take immense pride in the portfolio I've constructed thus far, my unwavering work ethic, and the unwavering commitment I've always displayed in my efforts to assist others to the best of my abilities.
